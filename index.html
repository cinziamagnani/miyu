SEA studios is an inclusive platform where people meet various worlds through art experience. SEA studios aims for Re-Humanization. SEA studios provides art therapeutic workshops, communication building, coaching, and creative web-design to diversify and enrich people’s perspectives and lives.

We offer you Art-based learning projects, workshops, or curriculum. We listen to your needs and design the best art programs for you. It is not teaching art techniques such as painting, but about how to foster students’ soft skills such as senses, self-esteem, observation skill, communication, expression, critical thinking, and problem solving through different topics. ▶Topic Ex: SDGs, Diversity/Inclusivity, Environment, Human Rights, Politics, Food, Fashions etc.
